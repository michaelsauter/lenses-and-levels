Architecture Documentation Templates

To help you get started quickly, we provide templates for the most common documentation platforms. Choose the format that fits your team's workflow.

Markdown Template

Ideal for Architecture-as-Code. Keep your documentation right alongside your source code in Git.

Confluence Template

Perfect for enterprise environments. Our guide helps you set up a Lenses & Levels space in Confluence.

Notion Template

The most flexible option. Includes built-in status tracking for your documentation progress.

Pro Tip: Living Documentation

Remember that for Level 3 (Elaboration), you should rarely write new text. Instead, use these templates to embed or link to your Swagger UI, Grafana Dashboards, or source code directories.
